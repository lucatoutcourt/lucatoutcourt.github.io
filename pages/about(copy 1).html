About

STF offers incomplete but interesting yet usable fonts, drawned by Luca Arnaud, a graphic design student at isdaT (France). However, I'm open to display and distribute any free student font, send me an message!

This is a hand-crafted website, please excuse any inconsistencies, responsive issues or errors. To ease the development of this website, and considering that the information and context about the fonts are more important than the exact wording used to share them, some uses of AI were made for parts of the code and certain text sections.
