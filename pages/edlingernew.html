A display font to climbB

Edlinger conveys a strong identity, suggesting the climbing world through rock shapes, but escaping the usual half-assumed figuration.

The radical drawings of the letterforms directly evokes boulder silhouettos, and are an invitation to a simple yet effective singular communication.

The character set has been kept simple, but provides useful and basic tools to satisfy common needs.
